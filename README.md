Overview

TaxEase is a web-based Nigerian Tax Calculator designed to simplify tax computations for employees, freelancers, and small business owners. The platform provides users with an easy way to estimate Personal Income Tax (PAYE), calculate deductions, and understand how their taxes are computed.

The goal of TaxEase is to make tax calculations transparent, accurate, and accessible to everyone without requiring advanced knowledge of tax laws or manual calculations.

---

Problem Statement

Many Nigerians find it difficult to understand how much tax they owe due to complex tax regulations, statutory deductions, and varying income structures. Manual calculations are often time-consuming and prone to errors.

TaxEase addresses this challenge by providing an automated and user-friendly solution that calculates taxes based on Nigerian Personal Income Tax regulations.

How It Works

1. User creates an account or logs in.
2. User enters income and deduction details.
3. TaxEase processes the information using Nigerian tax rules.
4. The system displays:
   - Gross Income
   - Taxable Income
   - PAYE Tax
   - Pension Contributions
   - Net Salary
5. Users can save records, make payments, and view payment history.
