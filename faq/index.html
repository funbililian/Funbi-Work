Tax Calculation

PAYE (Pay As You Earn) is a tax system where income tax is deducted directly from an employee's earnings before payment is made.

TaxEase calculates tax based on the earning and deduction information you provide, using the applicable Nigerian PAYE tax rules and rates configured on the platform.

Your net salary is the amount you receive after deductions such as PAYE tax, pension contributions, NHF contributions, and other applicable deductions.

Depending on your situation, you may include pension contributions, NHF contributions, and other allowable deductions supported by the platform.

CRA is a tax relief provided under Nigerian tax regulations that helps reduce the amount of income subject to tax.

Taxable income is the portion of your income that remains after eligible deductions and reliefs have been applied.
